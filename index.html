What We're Fighting For

Five core principles that drive every decision we make and every candidate we support.

01

Protect the NH Advantage

Opposing new taxes and regulations to maintain economic competitiveness. No income tax. No sales tax. That's the New Hampshire way.

02

Parental Rights

Empowering parents in educational and welfare decisions for their children through strong legislative protections.

03

Educational Freedom

Supporting school choice initiatives and removing barriers to educational opportunity for every Granite State family.

04

Lower Taxes

Advocating policies to stimulate economic growth, attract businesses, and create jobs while keeping more money in your pocket.

05

Safe Communities

Supporting law enforcement and defending Second Amendment and Article 2-a rights to keep New Hampshire communities the safest in the nation.
