Leadership

Meet the dedicated leaders working to elect a Republican majority in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.

Chairman & Majority Leader

Rep. Jason Osborne

Serves as the House Majority Leader in Concord and has been a State Representative since 2014. As Chairman, he leads the committee's efforts to recruit, support, and elect Republican candidates across New Hampshire.

Auburn, Chester & Sandown
Vice Chairman & Election Law Chairman

Rep. Ross Berry

Advises on the day-to-day operations of the Committee and serves as Chairman of the House Election Law Committee. Lives in Weare where he and his wife own and operate several small businesses.

Weare & Goffstown
Treasurer & Speaker Pro Tempore

Rep. Jim Kofalt

Part of the Speaker's leadership team for the New Hampshire House of Representatives. As Treasurer, he oversees the committee's financial operations and ensures responsible stewardship of donor contributions.

Wilton, New Ipswich & Temple
