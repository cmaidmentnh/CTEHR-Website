Our Pledge to You

Contract with New Hampshire

A comprehensive 10-point plan designed to address the key issues facing our state — from property tax reduction to energy costs, parental rights to public safety.

1

Housing

We promote local control while reducing state regulations to help New Hampshire families achieve homeownership. By cutting red tape and empowering municipalities, we can address the housing shortage while preserving community character.

2

Taxes & Spending

We are committed to opposing income and sales taxes, lowering the overall tax burden, and ensuring efficient spending with maximum effect and value. New Hampshire's tax advantage is the foundation of our prosperity.

3

Jobs & the Economy

Government shouldn't interfere with small businesses — the drivers of our economy. We will fight to reduce regulations, lower costs, and create an environment where New Hampshire businesses can thrive and grow.

4

Education

We prioritize the Parental Bill of Rights, demanding transparency and positioning parents and students at the top of the priority list. Every family deserves a say in their children's education.

5

Energy

We advocate for a robust, mixed delivery energy system through forward-looking innovation, not backroom deals. Lower energy costs mean a stronger economy and more money in Granite Staters' pockets.

6

Childcare

We target reduction of over 100 pages of regulations, focusing on worker advancement and recognizing that ninety percent of childcare centers are women-owned small businesses that deserve our support.

7

Healthcare

We propose deregulation and patient choice. By getting government out of the way, we can increase competition, lower costs, and put healthcare decisions back in the hands of patients and their doctors.

8

Support Our Police

We will always back the badge. From bail reform to law enforcement protection, we stand with the men and women who keep our communities safe every single day.

9

Abortion

Current New Hampshire law permits abortion for any reason during the first six months of pregnancy. Over 70% of Granite Staters agree with this standard, and we believe it reflects the will of the people.

10

Immigration

We oppose illegal immigration and sanctuary policies. New Hampshire should prioritize its residents and enforce immigration laws that protect our communities and uphold the rule of law.
